Winter Physics Camp 2025

Event Description

Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club presents Winter Physics Camp 2025 conducted by our honorable alumni. Designed to bridge foundational concepts with advanced problem-solving techniques.

Overall Session Time: 10:00 AM – 12:15 PM
Each day consists of two 1-hour sessions with a 15-minute break.

Camp Schedule & Topics

23 Dec 2025 Heat & Thermodynamics
24 Dec 2025 Work, Energy & Power
27 Dec 2025 Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM) & Waves
28 Dec 2025 Relativity & Quantum Mechanics
29 Dec 2025 Electricity & Magnetism, Newtonian Mechanics
30 Dec 2025 Winter Physics Mastery Test 2025 (Open Book Exam) & Wrap-Up

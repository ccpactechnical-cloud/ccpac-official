SESSIONS

Academic Sessions

CCPAC regularly conducts academic sessions on physics and astronomy, led by seniors, alumni, and invited instructors[cite: 8]. These sessions cover a wide range of topics, from fundamental concepts to advanced research, providing members with insights into higher studies, scientific thinking, and real-world applications[cite: 8]. Active participation is encouraged, allowing students to ask questions, engage in discussions, and explore ideas beyond textbooks[cite: 8].

Olympiad Sessions

To prepare members for local, national, and international physics competitions, CCPAC organizes dedicated Olympiad Sessions[cite: 8]. These sessions focus on problem-solving techniques, conceptual clarity, and analytical skills[cite: 8]. Members receive guidance from experienced seniors and alumni who have excelled in various Olympiads, helping them develop a competitive edge and deepen their understanding of physics[cite: 8].

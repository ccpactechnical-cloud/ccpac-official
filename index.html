Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club

"Promoting scientific literacy from subatomic scales to galactic horizons"

Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is a student-driven community where curiosity meets the cosmos. We strive to make sense of the universe in unconventional ways. Our mission is to shift your perspective from mere sensory impressions to a deeper understanding of reality.

At CCPAC, we explore the fundamental laws of nature and the vast mysteries that lie beyond our skies. From the tiniest particles to the grandest galaxies, we learn to question, observe, and understand together.

Membership

Who Can Join?

Membership at CCPAC is open to all students of Chittagong College with an interest in physics and astronomy. Curiosity matters more than prior expertise.

Why Join CCPAC?

  • Academic sessions led by seniors and alumni.
  • Problem-solving skills through Weekly Physics Challenges.
  • Scientific writing and research guidance.

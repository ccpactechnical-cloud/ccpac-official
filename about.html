About CCPAC

"Promoting scientific literacy from subatomic scales to galactic horizons"

Who We Are

Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club (CCPAC) is a student-driven academic community where curiosity meets the cosmos. Founded under the glorious tradition of Chittagong College, we strive to make sense of the universe in unconventional ways. Our mission is to shift your perspective from mere sensory impressions to a deeper, mathematical, and logical understanding of reality.

At CCPAC, we explore the fundamental laws of nature and the vast mysteries that lie beyond our skies. From the tiniest subatomic particles to the grandest galaxies, we learn to question, observe, and experiment together through study circles, workshops, problem-solving classes, and interactive segments like our podcast EigenTalks and Weekly Physics Challenge.

Our Vision

To cultivate a dynamic intellectual community of young scientists and astronomers at Chittagong College who possess analytical prowess, boundless cosmic curiosity, and the capability to excel on national and global stages.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between classroom textbooks and advanced scientific exploration through peer mentoring, Olympiad training, collaborative problem-solving, and scientific writing.

About CCPAC

Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is a premier student-run scientific community dedicated to nurturing deep scientific curiosity, critical thinking, and cosmic exploration.

Our Vision

To build a intellectually vibrant community of young minds at Chittagong College who look beyond conventional boundaries, master foundational physics, gaze into the deepest realms of astronomy, and emerge as future pioneers of science and innovation.

Our Mission

  • Demystify complex physical laws and astronomical phenomena through interactive study circles and workshops.
  • Prepare enthusiastic students for National and International Physics, Astronomy, and Mathematics Olympiads.
  • Foster scientific writing, problem-solving prowess, and collaborative research ethics.
Our Legacy

Rooted in Excellence at Chittagong College

Established under the glorious academic heritage of Chittagong College, CCPAC has continually served as a beacon for passionate science learners. From organizing flagship physics camps, stargazing sessions, and podcast discussions to achieving glorious milestones on national and international stages (such as International Mathematical Olympiad achievements), our club represents unwavering dedication.

We believe that true learning happens when curiosity meets action. Through peer-to-peer mentoring, rigorous problem-solving sessions, and interactive academic discussions, CCPAC transforms inquisitive students into confident researchers, thinkers, and problem solvers.

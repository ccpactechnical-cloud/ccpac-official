Who We Are?

Rooted in a timeless curiosity to understand nature and the cosmos, the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club was founded on 13 February 2025. Although it was established on this date, its official journey truly began on November 22, 2025, through the formation of its first executive panel—the Executive Committee 2025–26. CCPAC aspires to cultivate a dynamic academic community for students passionate about physics and astronomy, promoting scientific literacy and curiosity by exploring fundamental laws of nature and the vast mysteries of the universe.

CCPAC serves as a hub for learning, collaboration, and intellectual growth beyond the classroom. We regularly organize academic sessions on physics and astronomy, conducted by senior students and alumni who are currently studying at various renowned universities.

To further extend scientific dialogue and expression, CCPAC highlights its own podcast EigenTalks, a Weekly Physics Challenge, and an engaging Article Writing segment to strengthen analytical thinking, scientific writing, and problem-solving skills among members.

The CCPAC Family

The Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is more than an academic organization; it is a family united by a shared passion for physics and astronomy.

This family is formed by the Executive Committee, batchmates, juniors, seniors, alumni, and instructors. Batchmates grow together through collaboration and shared learning. Juniors are nurtured with guidance, while seniors lead with responsibility and experience.

The CCPAC family is built on mutual respect, cooperation, and a common purpose: to explore the universe and understand the laws of nature together. Here, every member has a role, and every question is welcomed.

Welcome to CCPAC

Who We Are?

Rooted in a timeless curiosity to understand nature and the cosmos, the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club was established on 13 February 2025. Following its inception, the club formally commenced its administrative journey on November 22, 2025, marked by the establishment of the Executive Committee 2025–26 as its inaugural leadership body. CCPAC aspires to cultivate a vibrant academic community dedicated to physics and astronomy, fostering scientific literacy and inquiry through the exploration of fundamental natural laws and cosmic mysteries.

Serving as a premier platform for intellectual enrichment beyond the traditional curriculum, CCPAC organizes regular academic seminars, specialized camps, and interactive events led by distinguished alumni and senior scholars currently pursuing higher education at esteemed institutions.

To further extend scientific dialogue and expression, CCPAC highlights its own podcast EigenTalks, a Weekly Physics Challenge, and an engaging Article Writing segment, while maintaining a proud tradition of academic excellence through our comprehensive The Achievers' Blueprint.

The CCPAC Family

The Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is more than an academic organization; it is a family united by a shared passion for physics and astronomy.

This family is formed by the Executive Committee, batchmates, juniors, seniors, alumni, and instructors. Batchmates grow together through collaboration and shared learning. Juniors are nurtured with guidance, while seniors lead with responsibility and experience.

The CCPAC family is built on mutual respect, cooperation, and a common purpose: to explore the universe and understand the laws of nature together. Here, every member has a role, and every question is welcomed.

About CCPAC

Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is a premier student-run scientific community dedicated to nurturing deep scientific curiosity, critical thinking, and cosmic exploration.

Who We Are

Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club

Rooted in a timeless curiosity to understand nature and the cosmos, the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club, founded on 13 February 2025, aspires to cultivate a dynamic academic community for students passionate about physics and astronomy. Our mission is to promote scientific literacy and curiosity by exploring the fundamental laws of nature and the vast mysteries of the universe.

CCPAC serves as a hub for learning, collaboration, and intellectual growth beyond the classroom. We regularly organize academic sessions on physics and astronomy, conducted by senior students and alumni who are currently studying at various renowned universities.

To further extend scientific dialogue and expression, CCPAC highlights its own podcast EigenTalks, a Weekly Physics Challenge, and an engaging Article Writing segment to strengthen analytical thinking, scientific writing, and problem-solving skills among members.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant and intellectually fearless community of young minds at Chittagong College who look beyond conventional boundaries, master foundational physics, gaze into the deepest realms of astronomy, and emerge as future pioneers of science, research, and innovation for the nation and the world.

Our Mission

To cultivate scientific literacy, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills through regular academic study circles, Olympiad camps, engaging podcasts like EigenTalks, and collaborative practical projects, bridging the gap between classroom textbooks and the wonders of the cosmos.

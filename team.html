CCPAC Executive Panel

The Executive Panel of the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club plays a vital role in guiding the club’s activities and ensuring its smooth operation. This panel is responsible for planning, organizing, and supervising all academic and organizational programs of the club.

The Executive Panel works with dedication to promote physics and astronomy among students. They arrange regular academic sessions, coordinate with seniors and alumni for lectures and discussions, and manage weekly activities such as the Weekly Physics Challenge and article writing programs. Their efforts help create a structured and engaging environment for learning beyond the classroom.

Through teamwork and leadership, the Executive Panel ensures that every member gets opportunities to participate and grow. They also maintain discipline, communication, and cooperation within the club, strengthening the bond among juniors, seniors, and alumni.

The CCPAC Executive Panel is not only an administrative body but also a group of students committed to inspiring curiosity, encouraging scientific thinking, and building a strong academic community. Their leadership continues to guide CCPAC toward its goal of exploring the universe through knowledge and collaboration.

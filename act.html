What We Do

Our Activities

At the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club, we engage our members through a diverse range of activities designed to nurture curiosity, scientific thinking, and problem-solving skills. We organize regular academic sessions on physics and astronomy, conducted by seniors, alumni, and invited instructors, covering topics from foundational concepts to advanced research, while also providing valuable insights into higher studies and real-world applications.

To further strengthen analytical abilities and critical thinking, CCPAC conducts a Weekly Physics Challenge, where members engage with carefully curated problems that encourage logical reasoning and consistent interaction with physical concepts.

In addition, the club actively promotes scientific communication through article writing initiatives, enabling members to articulate ideas, reflect on sessions, and present complex concepts in a clear and engaging manner.

Expanding beyond traditional formats, CCPAC has also introduced its own podcast, EigenTalks, which serves as a platform for meaningful conversations, where academic insights, personal journeys, and diverse perspectives come together.

Through this blend of academic and creative initiatives, members not only deepen their understanding of science but also develop collaboration, creativity, and a lasting passion for exploration—making CCPAC a vibrant and intellectually stimulating community.

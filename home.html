Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club

"Promoting scientific literacy from subatomic scales to galactic horizons"

Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is a student-driven community where curiosity meets the cosmos. We strive to make sense of the universe in unconventional ways. Our mission is to shift your perspective from mere sensory impressions to a deeper understanding of reality.

At CCPAC, we explore the fundamental laws of nature and the vast mysteries that lie beyond our skies. From the tiniest particles to the grandest galaxies, we learn to question, observe, and understand together. CCPAC is a home for minds that refuse to stop wondering, for eyes that look at the universe and ask how and why, and for hearts that seek meaning through science.

You are no longer just an observer of nature; you are now part of its exploration. Step into this journey of inquiry, and let the universe reveal its stories to you.

Membership

Who Can Join?

Membership at the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is open to all students of Chittagong College who have an interest in physics and astronomy. We believe that curiosity is more important than prior expertise.

Whether you are just beginning your journey in science or already passionate about understanding the universe, CCPAC provides a welcoming space for learning and growth.

Why Join CCPAC?

  • Gain exposure to physics and astronomy through regular academic sessions led by seniors and alumni.
  • Improve your problem-solving and critical thinking skills through the Weekly Physics Challenge.
  • Develop scientific writing and communication skills through article writing activities.
  • Connect with like-minded students who share an interest in science and exploration.
  • Receive guidance and inspiration for higher studies and research in science-related fields.

Executive Panel 2025–26

The Executive Panel 2025–26 of the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club serves as the core administrative and leadership committee driving all club operations. This active panel orchestrates academic programs, manages public relations, and directs core initiatives.

Under their tenure, the club successfully coordinates multiple hallmark activities including regular academic Sessions, the Weekly Physics Challenge, insightful Articles, flagship events like Winter Camp 2025, and interactive discussions featured in the EigenTalks Podcast.
